Greetings PCHB!
Today we launch Coffee Talk, a computer application that simulates picking a name out of a hat. This is a new bureau initiative brought to you by the Bureau Development Work Group and a fun way to get to know your bureau colleagues. Many of us don’t see each other on a day-to-day basis since we’re located in different areas of NM. We hope this brings staff in the bureau closer together. We believe that the more we get to know each other, the easier it is to reach out and work together.
Here’s how it works:
  • When you enter the site, you’ll be able to push a button and a random bureau colleague’s name will be “magically” revealed to you.
  • You then call or email that person and find a time to have a conversation to get to know one another. This is considered work time and you can meet face-to-face, via skype, have a phone conversation, take a walk, get a cup of coffee, etc.! You decide. You can talk about anything you like. If you’re unsure how to get the conversation started, you’ll also receive a fun question that may help you get going. You choose whether you want to use that question or not.
  • You’ll have an opportunity to talk with one of your bureau colleagues twice each quarter, once as an initiator and once as a recipient when someone else gets your name. The list of names will get refreshed (re-randomized) every quarter, and everyone can pick again!
  • Have fun with it!
